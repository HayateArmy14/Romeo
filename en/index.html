Welcome to Romeo

At the foot of the Castle, where the Sea touches the Earth, Romeo Restaurant is located. Since 1992 it is using only the finest products of Limnos' soil and the superb fish and seafood of the Aegean Sea in order to offer, to all customers, authentic Greek and Mediterranean dishes.
